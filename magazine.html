About

I designed this magazine regarding on the appreciation of traditional Chinese wedding ceremony. In fact, there are still many people in China use the traditional ceremony instead of Western wedding ceremony. This Magazine also includes many Chinese traditional culture stories that are related to the wedding. For example, the Gaitou Culture and the Wedding bed setting. Last but not least, it includes a side bar telling some tips for a foreigner who is going to attend a Chinese wedding.
